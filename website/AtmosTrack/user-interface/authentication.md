# How to Log Into AtmosTrack

AtmosTrack offers two convenient ways to access the system, designed to fit your hospital's workflow and security requirements.

## Login Options

### Microsoft Login (Recommended for Administrative Tasks)

Use this method when:
- You're setting up the system for the first time
- You need administrative access
- Your hospital uses Microsoft Office or Teams
- You're working on reports or configuration

**How to use Microsoft Login:**
1. Click the "Login with Microsoft" button
2. You'll be redirected to your familiar Microsoft login page
3. Enter your hospital email and password
4. Complete any multi-factor authentication if required
5. You'll be automatically logged into AtmosTrack

**Benefits:**
- Uses your existing hospital Microsoft account
- Secure single sign-on
- Automatic password management
- Works with your hospital's security policies

### Barcode Login (Best for Daily Operations)

Use this method when:
- You're working in the CSSD processing instruments
- You need quick access during busy periods
- You're using a barcode scanner regularly
- You want fast login without typing

**How to use Barcode Login:**
1. Click "Login with Barcode" button
2. Scan your employee badge with your barcode scanner
3. Enter your personal PIN code
4. You're immediately logged in and ready to work

**Benefits:**
- Lightning-fast access in under 10 seconds
- No typing required - just scan and enter PIN
- Perfect for busy hospital environments
- Works with any standard barcode scanner

## Language Selection

Before logging in, you can choose your preferred language:
- **English** - Default language
- **Suomi** - Finnish for Finnish hospitals
- **Eesti** - Estonian for Estonian facilities
- **العربية** - Arabic with right-to-left text support

Simply click the language dropdown in the top-right corner to select your preference.

## First-Time Setup

### Getting Your Barcode Login Credentials

Your IT administrator will provide:
1. **Employee Barcode**: Usually your existing hospital ID badge
2. **Personal PIN**: A secure 4-6 digit code just for you
3. **Initial Training**: Quick walkthrough of the system

### Setting Up Your Microsoft Account

If using Microsoft login:
1. Your hospital IT team will configure your account
2. Use the same email and password you use for other hospital systems
3. Multi-factor authentication may be required for security

## Staying Logged In

### Session Management
- AtmosTrack keeps you logged in during your shift
- Your session remains active while you use the system
- You'll be automatically logged out after extended inactivity for security

### Switching Between Devices
- Log in on any computer or tablet in your hospital
- Your work and preferences sync across all devices
- No need to log out when moving between workstations

### Automatic Updates
- AtmosTrack automatically refreshes your login credentials
- You won't be interrupted during busy periods
- The system handles security updates in the background

## Troubleshooting Login Issues

### Microsoft Login Problems

**"Cannot access Microsoft account":**
- Check with your IT department
- Ensure you're using your hospital email address
- Verify multi-factor authentication is working

**"Redirect error":**
- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Try using a different browser
- Contact IT support if the problem continues

### Barcode Login Problems

**"Employee barcode not recognized":**
- Ensure your badge barcode is clean and undamaged
- Try manually entering the numbers below the barcode
- Check with your supervisor that your employee ID is activated

**"Invalid PIN":**
- Double-check you're entering the correct PIN
- Contact your supervisor if you've forgotten your PIN
- Your PIN may need to be reset by an administrator

**Scanner not working:**
- Ensure the scanner is properly connected
- Try manually typing your employee ID
- Check that the scanner light turns on when scanning

## Security Best Practices

### Protecting Your Login

**For Microsoft Login:**
- Never share your hospital email password
- Always log out when leaving your workstation
- Report suspicious login attempts to IT

**For Barcode Login:**
- Keep your PIN confidential
- Don't let others use your employee badge
- Report lost or damaged badges immediately
- Change your PIN if you suspect it's been compromised

### Safe Usage

**During Your Shift:**
- Lock your screen when stepping away
- Don't leave AtmosTrack open on unattended computers
- Log out completely at the end of your shift

**Multi-User Workstations:**
- Always verify you're logged in as yourself
- Log out the previous user if they forgot
- Use your own credentials, never share accounts
