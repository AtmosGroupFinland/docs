# Using AtmosTrack in Your Hospital

AtmosTrack is your complete solution for managing surgical instruments and containers throughout the sterilization process. This guide will help you understand how to use AtmosTrack to improve efficiency and safety in your Central Sterile Supply Department (CSSD).

## What AtmosTrack Does for You

AtmosTrack helps your hospital team:
- Track every surgical instrument from dirty to clean
- Ensure complete instrument sets before surgery
- Monitor sterilization equipment performance
- Reduce lost instruments and improve patient safety
- Speed up turnaround times between surgeries

## Getting Started

### Logging In
AtmosTrack offers two convenient ways to log in:

- **Microsoft Login**: Use your hospital's existing Microsoft account
- **Quick Barcode Login**: Scan your employee badge and enter your PIN for fast access

### Main Dashboard
When you first log in, you'll see your personalized dashboard showing:

- **Today's Progress**: How many containers you've processed
- **Equipment Status**: Which sterilizers and washers are running
- **Department Performance**: How well each operating room is meeting targets
- **Quality Metrics**: Your department's success rates

## Daily Workflows

### CSSD Management
View and manage your department structure:

- **See All Departments**: View containers organized by operating room or department
- **Track Container Status**: Know which containers are clean, dirty, or in process
- **Manage Locations**: Keep track of where containers are located

### Packaging Workflow
The heart of AtmosTrack - preparing instrument sets:

- **Scan Containers**: Use your barcode scanner to select a container
- **Add Instruments**: Scan each instrument as you place it in the container
- **Visual Confirmation**: See exactly which instruments are included
- **Check Completeness**: AtmosTrack warns you if instruments are missing

### Monitor Performance
Track your department's efficiency:

- **Processing Volume**: See how many containers you handle daily
- **Turnaround Times**: Monitor how quickly containers move through the system
- **Equipment Performance**: Check sterilizer cycles and uptime
- **Quality Scores**: Review compliance and accuracy rates
